# ZubairRay Quick CTA Bar

A lightweight WordPress plugin that adds a sticky call to action bar to your website with Phone, WhatsApp, Email, and Custom URL buttons.

Perfect for businesses, agencies, freelancers, WooCommerce stores, consultants, and service providers who want to increase leads, customer engagement, and conversions.

## Installation

1. Upload the plugin to your WordPress website.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. Navigate to Settings → ZubairRay Quick CTA Bar.
4. Configure your CTA bar settings.
5. Save changes.

## Screenshots

### General Settings
Configure bar position and enable or disable the plugin.

### Content Settings
Customize message text, CTA text, and CTA actions.

### Design Settings
Control colors, spacing, height, and button appearance.

### Visibility Settings
Choose who can see the CTA bar and on which devices.

### Advanced Settings
Configure close button behavior and additional options.
